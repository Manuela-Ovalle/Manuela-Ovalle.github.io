Kia ora!
My name is Manuela, I was born in Colombia and raised in beautiful Tamaki Makaurau. I've worn several different job hats sice completing my undergrad studies at Auckland University but Musician has been my main role. I think of myself as a maker, I love making creative things and equiping myself with the skills needed to make more stuff. I am passionate about designing, illustrating and creating digital resources that help myself and other creative people around me promote their music and to learn new things. I am incredibly excited and ethusiastic to learn to code, it's a superpower in my eyes and I think it will enable me to use my craftswomanship efficiently and dive into a world I didn't know existed.
